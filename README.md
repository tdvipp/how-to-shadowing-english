# how-to-shadowing-english

Source GEMINI

Step-by-Step Shadowing Routine:
1. Select a 1-to-5 minute clip of a native speaker, such as a podcast, YouTube video, or TV show. Pick content where the speaker’s natural pace is clear.
2. Listen for Comprehension: Play the audio once without speaking. Focus entirely on the meaning, emotion, and general flow.
3. Review the Transcript: Read along with the text while listening to the audio again. Highlight words that are difficult or linked together unnaturally.
4. Speak (Echo): Play the audio again and repeat out loud just a fraction of a second behind the speaker. Try to match their exact rhythm, pauses, and inflections.
5. Record and Refine: Record yourself shadowing a short segment. Listen to the playback and compare your pronunciation to the original audio.
